HTML5 New Tag [SVG - Canvas] Comparing Concept:

=> [SVG] Tag needs alot of Lessons and Trainging to achieve the Mastering of its amazing Features, and in this course we had only the basics.

=> [SVG - Canvas] => We will have a comparison based on following Cretearia:

  • Quality of Zooming and Resolution:
    • [SVG]:

      • Provides high Quality in Zooming with out any pixilation problems.

    • [Canvas]:

      • Zooming will cause a Pixilation Technical Problems and low Quality.

  • Rendering Performance :
    • [SVG]:

      • High Speed in Rendering .

    • [Canvas]:

      • Low Speed in Rendering .

  • The best suiting Usages and Applications of each Technology:
    • [SVG]:

      • Suitable with Creating and designing Logos and Slogans and Maps (due to its endless Scalable zooming) such as [Google Maps] applications.

    • [Canvas]:

      • Suitable with Creating designing animiating Shapes and Lines such as [Web - Games] Applications .
