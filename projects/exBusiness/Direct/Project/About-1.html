ABOUT OUR COMPANY

DIERCT Construction and Project Management is a group of highly qualified and experienced professionals committed to delivering high-quality projects and quality engineering services while providing multi-disciplinary technical solutions to meet our clients' satisfaction. Together, we embrace our core values of trust and consistency that have allowed us to complete numerous projects in the hospitality, healthcare, industrial, education, commercial and residential sectors. At the heart of these projects is a commitment to sustainability, achievement and leadership. We are at the forefront of delivering healthy, efficient and cost-effective LEED certified projects that help build and develop equitable and green communities. We build our business on responsibility, respect and forward-thinking. With our partners and customers, we are proud to work together to build the communities of the future. We are a multi-purpose company that encourages diversity and authenticity in the workforce to achieve common goals. Creating a well-defined sphere in which different ideas can be freely expressed in pursuit of a consistent goal is essential to creating a harmonious and receptive working environment, an environment that has enabled DIERCT to embrace various innovations

OUR VISION

We are providing a complete technical smart engineering solutions for electromechanical systems which meet or exceed our customer satisfaction with high quality & competitive price To deliver all our projects with an uncompromising commitment to our customers’ needs while exceeding their expectations through the application of cutting-edge technologies, processes and practices

OUR CULTURE

Our work culture is embodied in a number of principles and values that guide every ASGC employee in delivering our vision:

  • Foster a professional & ethical work environment.
  • Set safety and quality as our top priorities.
  • Encourage creativity & innovation in every aspect our work
  • Recognize the value of continuous improvement
  • Be open, listen to our customers, & adapt to change

COMPANY MISSION

To present all of our projects with an uncompromising commitment to the needs of our clients while exceeding their expectations by applying the latest technologies, processes and practices.
