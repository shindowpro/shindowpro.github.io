We are WS Group

No.1 Events Management

Our Mission

Providing exhibition and event organization services, consultancy, marketing and administrative services to our partners through our deep understanding of local culture, adoption of international best practices and continuous analysis of the market and diverse customer needs.

Our Vission

Achieving leadership in the Middle East in the field of organizing exhibitions and events through the integration of our services, innovation in technical solutions, creativity in our administrative practice and continuous development.

Who We Are

We are an Exhibition and event management service agency, specialized in providing exhibition and event management services within the highest standards that include high-level values for our partners. What distinguishes us in this field is the integration and comprehensiveness of the main support services that we provide under one roof. Exhibition and event management, which includes media, marketing and production services. And promotion, in addition to providing administrative and marketing advisory services.

Our Value

We dedicate our commitment to the values of our culture to serve as the moral motivation in all stages of planning and implementation of work.

Our clients is our partners

Our customers are our partners and our real Value, so we need them to grow their business, and growing their business is the ultimate goal for us and the priority given in all our business.

Development and innovation

On Wall Street, We constantly develop our services, our work style, our skills, and our capabilities to maximize the values offered to our customers.
