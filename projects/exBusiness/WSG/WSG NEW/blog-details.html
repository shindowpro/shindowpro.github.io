Featured Event

Kempinski Nile Hotel,Cairo Egypt National Expo
10/2021

  • Date

    10 Octobor 2021

  • Category

    Exhibitions

The activities of the Egypt National Exhibition launched in October 16, with the participation of more than 40 real estate development companies at the Kempinski Hotel - Fifth Settlement. The real estate companies participating in the exhibition present various projects in the New Administrative Capital.

Eng. Elsayed CEO of WS Group

Elsayed, CEO of Wall Street Company, the organizing company of the exhibition, stated that in light of the new republic and the transformation that Egypt is witnessing in all fields, and under direct management by a national political leadership, it is strongly pushing to put Egypt in an advanced position worthy of it in all fields and in the context of what the real estate market is witnessing from An unprecedented growth and boom in the length and breadth of the country, which comes at the forefront of the new administrative capital, the luminous jewel of Egypt in modernity and urban planning, which is one of the fourth generation cities, prompted the company to establish the Egypt National Exhibition.

Elsayed explained that, the increase in demand and the great interest from citizens and customers in all types of housing and administrative units and investment opportunities in the new administrative capital, whose implementation rates are close to completion and the delivery of the first phase, which prompted the company to prepare and organize Egypt's National Exhibition, which includes major companies and real estate developers in the capital.

Ms. Mirna Adel Sales Manager of WS Group :

Mirna Adel, the company's sales manager, said that the Egypt National Exhibition is a real opportunity for ownership and investment in the capital, given the offers and facilities in the payment and a great diversity of residential and commercial units. It is noteworthy that the Wall Street Company's organization of the Egypt National Exhibition is the result of more than ten years of experience working in the field of real estate marketing and contracting.
